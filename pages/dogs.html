Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever or simply Labrador is a British breed of retriever gun dog.
It was developed in the United Kingdom from fishing dogs imported from the colony of Newfoundland (now a province of Canada),
and was named after the Labrador region of that colony.
It is among the most commonly kept dogs in several countries,
particularly in the Western world.

Labradors favorite things in order!

  1. Eating
  2. Swimming
  3. Social Gatherings
